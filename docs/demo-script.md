# DG Capability Factory Demo Script

## Purpose

Use this script to demonstrate DG Capability Factory as a client-ready DG Academy offer. The demo should show a complete operating workflow: idea, proposal, pricing, pipeline, delivery, quality, governance, and client portal.

## 10-Minute Demo Flow

1. Open `/product`.
2. Introduce the promise: one system for training package production, proposal pipeline, delivery, quality, and governance.
3. Open `/packages/new` and show the training idea form.
4. Generate or open the demo package `[DEMO] AI Skills for Managers`.
5. Show output tabs: syllabus, proposal, workbook, slides, follow-up, pricing, QA.
6. Show export buttons and explain DOCX, PPTX, and PDF outputs.
7. Open `/pipeline` and show the demo opportunity moving from proposal to delivery.
8. Open `/clients`, select the demo client, and show the client portal panel.
9. Explain client-safe publishing and feedback collection.
10. Close with `/roi-calculator` and the product brief export.

## 20-Minute Demo Flow

1. Product framing on `/product`.
2. Create or open demo workspace data.
3. Generate a training package from a real client scenario.
4. Review pricing assumptions and deterministic calculations.
5. Export the proposal DOCX and slide outline PPTX.
6. Create or link an opportunity.
7. Show pipeline dashboard metrics and follow-up draft generation.
8. Open delivery project and show checklist, trainer prep, evaluation, and report.
9. Open knowledge base and explain client-safe versus internal knowledge.
10. Open quality dashboard and evals.
11. Open security dashboard and explain internal margin/export protection.
12. Open client portal and demonstrate published client-safe review.
13. Use ROI calculator.
14. Export product brief.
15. Discuss implementation packages and rollout.

## Objection Handling

**We already use ChatGPT.**
DG Capability Factory turns ad hoc prompting into a governed workflow with saved packages, pricing, CRM, delivery, exports, feedback, and quality loops.

**We worry about confidentiality.**
The system separates internal knowledge from client-safe outputs, blocks internal margin exposure, and uses audit/security checks.

**Our training process is unique.**
The knowledge base, prompt templates, delivery checklist, and commercial templates can be configured to the client's workflows.

**We are not ready for agentic automation.**
Start with the Starter or Professional package. Orchestrator and self-improvement workflows can remain disabled until governance is ready.

**How do we prove ROI?**
Use the ROI calculator with proposal volume, staff cost, time saved, and training revenue supported.
